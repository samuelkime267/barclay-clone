You can register if you

  • Live in the uk

  • are 16 or older

  • Have a Barclays current or savings account with a bank card, or a Barclays mortgage

Accounts that need more than one signature

Your existing Barclays accounts will appear in Online Banking when you register, but accounts that need the signature of more than one person won’t be shown.
