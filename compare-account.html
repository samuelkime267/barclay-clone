    Current accounts

    Find a current account that works for you

    Everyday current accounts

    Barclays Bank Account

    No monthly cost

    Our standard and most popular account – it’s flexible and tailored to your needs.

    What you'll get

    • Optional arranged overdrafts
    • Rewards for banking with us
    • Manage your money with the Barclays app1, Online Banking and more

    You'll need to

    • Be aged 18 or over
    • Be a UK resident

    Premier Bank Account

    No monthly cost

    With Premier, you’ll get access to a range of exclusive products and benefits.

    What you'll get

    • Dedicated team of Financial Guides and access to 24/7 servicing support
    • Exclusive rates on Premier mortgages and savings accounts
    • Collect Avios with Barclays Avios Rewards or earn cash rewards with Barclays Blue Rewards, both for a monthly fee

    You'll need to

    • Be aged 18 or over
    • Be a UK resident
    • Have a minimum £75,000 annual gross income, or £100,000 saved or invested with us

    Open a current account straight from the Barclays app.

    Student and graduate current accounts

    error

    If you’ve lived in the UK for less than 3 years

    Unfortunately, you can’t apply to a Student Additions Account if you haven’t been a UK resident for 3+ years – but you can still apply for a Barclays Bank Account in the Barclays app.

    Student Additions Account

    No monthly cost

    Get more from your everyday banking with our account designed for student life.

    What you get

    • Apply for an interest-free overdraft up to £1,500 over the course of your studies
    • A free 12-month subscription to Perlego, the UK’s most popular e-book service for students

    To apply, you must

    • Be aged 18 or over
    • Have lived in the UK for at least 3 years
    • Be studying full-time in the UK
    • Be studying for an undergraduate degree (2 years’ minimum length)
      • or
    • Studying for a postgraduate degree (1-year minimum length)
      • or
    • Studying for a Higher Apprenticeship (Level 4+)

    Higher Education Account

    No monthly cost

    Whether you’re looking to start your first job or travel the world, take the next step with this account – available for three years after you graduate.

    What you'll get

    • No hidden charges
    • Optional arranged overdrafts
    • Add packs to your account – including our Tech Pack and Travel Pack

    You'll need to

    • Be aged 18 or over
    • Be a UK resident
    • Have completed a higher education programme in the last three years

    Accounts for under 18s

    Children’s Account – BarclayPlus

    No monthly cost

    Start your banking journey with our simple, secure account for 11 to 15 year olds.

    What you'll get

    • A cash card or debit card
    • A dedicated BarclayPlus app3 – specially designed for children
    • Buy things with your phone, using Apple Pay4 or Contactless Mobile

    You'll need to

    • Be aged 11 to 15
    • Be a UK resident
    • Get a parent or guardian to provide ID for you

    Young Person’s Account

    No monthly cost

    Spend, save and learn more about money with our account for 16 to 17 year olds.

    What you'll get

    • A free account with no hidden fees
    • Use Apple Pay to buy things with your phone
    • Manage your money on the go

    You'll need to

    • Be aged 16 or 17
    • Be a UK resident

    Foreign currency current accounts

    Foreign Currency Account

    No monthly cost

    Make and receive foreign payments with our foreign currency accounts.

    What you'll get

    • Reduced exchange rate risk
    • Cash deposits and withdrawals
    • 12 currencies available

    You'll need to

    • Be aged 18 or over
    • Have a sterling current account with us already

    Not sure you’re eligible?

    If you’re not eligible for our current accounts, don’t yet have a UK account, or you’re experiencing financial difficulty, a Barclays Basic Account could be right for you.
